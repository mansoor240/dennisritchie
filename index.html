Dennis MacAlistair Ritchie (September 9, 1941 – October 12, 2011)

Dennis Ritchie was an American computer scientist who created the C programming language and co-developed the UNIX operating system.
Ritchie was the head of Lucent Technologies System Software Research Department when he retired in 2007
Dennis Ritchie was born in Bronxville, New York.
Ritchie began working at the Bell Labs Computing Science Research Center
Ritchie was awarded with many awards for his contributions to computer systems
